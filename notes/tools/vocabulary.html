In a gist 总而言之 iteration and innovation stall 迭代和创新停滞
In a nutshell 简而言之
in the big picture 总体来看 no knowledge of them is required 不需要了解
kick in 起作用
kick off 开始
from scratch 从头开始
out of the box 开箱即用
stay tuned 敬请关注 ship features and deliver business value 交付功能和呈现业务价值
